# Unhired.fun — MVP

## Product

A free, anonymous job-search misery leaderboard.

> Still unemployed? At least get ranked.

Players answer a short questionnaire, receive an Unhired Score, join the most relevant league, unlock badges, and share a result card.

## MVP pages

1. Landing page
2. Three-minute scoring questionnaire
3. Result and share-card page
4. Global leaderboard
5. League leaderboard
6. Scoring methodology
7. Privacy and profile deletion

## Primary leagues

- Internship
- New Graduate
- Layoff
- Leap of Faith
- Career Switcher
- Comeback
- Open Unemployment

## Core features

- deterministic score from `UNHIRED-SCORING.md`;
- nickname-based anonymous profile;
- opt-in leaderboard;
- country and industry filters;
- badges;
- square and story-format share cards;
- return link for updating counts;
- scoring version stored with each result;
- report and delete controls.

## Viral loop

1. Calculate score.
2. Receive a funny rank and specific badge.
3. Compare within a fair league.
4. Share card.
5. Friends calculate their scores.
6. Player returns after another rejection, interview, or month.

## Monetization

The game remains free.

- one native sponsor card after results;
- one sponsor slot between leaderboard groups;
- optional job-board, CV-tool, or interview-product affiliate links;
- no interstitials, autoplay video, or ads inside the questionnaire;
- sponsors never influence scores or rankings.

## MVP data policy

- no legal name;
- no email required;
- no uploaded rejection letters;
- no public employer or school by default;
- sensitive demographic attributes are not collected for scoring;
- leaderboard participation requires separate consent;
- local recovery token permits editing/deletion without an account.

## Success metrics

- questionnaire completion rate;
- result-card share rate;
- visitor-to-player conversion;
- return rate within 30 days;
- percentage opting into a leaderboard;
- referrals generated per shared result.

Initial validation:

- 1,000 completed scores;
- at least 15% result-card share rate;
- at least 0.5 referred players per sharing player;
- at least 20% 30-day update rate among saved profiles.

## Scope exclusions

- job application tracker;
- CV builder;
- AI career coach;
- job matching;
- email inbox access;
- employer reviews;
- direct messaging;
- paid score boosts;
- subscription plans.

The first version is a game, not another employment platform.
